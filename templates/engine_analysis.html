About Engine Analysis

This feature uses Stockfish chess engine to deeply analyze your games and provide:

  • Centipawn Loss: Measures how much advantage you lose with each move
  • Accuracy by Phase: Opening, Middlegame, and Endgame accuracy scores
  • Blunder Detection: Automatically identifies blunders, mistakes, and inaccuracies
  • Opening Analysis: ECO codes and opening names

Note: Analysis takes 10-30 seconds per game depending on game length.
